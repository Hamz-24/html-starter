FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions):
1. How do I change my Vibe Mode (Dark Mode) setting? ⌄
Go to Settings → Appearance → Toggle "Vibe Mode" to enable or disable dark mode. Your preference is saved automatically.
2. What are the daily transfer limits? ⌄
Standard accounts: ₹1,00,000/day via NEFT/IMPS. Premium accounts: ₹5,00,000/day. Contact your branch to upgrade your limit.
3. I forgot my Login PIN or Password. What should I do? ⌄
Tap "Forgot Password" on the login screen. Enter your registered mobile number to receive an OTP and reset your PIN.
4. How do I add a new beneficiary? ⌄
Go to Transfer → Beneficiary → Add New. Enter the account holder's name, account number, and IFSC code. Verification takes up to 30 minutes.
